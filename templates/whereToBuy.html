Where to Buy?

It has never been easier to purchase Bitcoin or any other cryprocurrency than it is today. Keep in mind though, that investing in cryptocurrency is risky and that you shouldn't invest more than what you can afford to lose.

If you want to own bitcoin as quickly as possible, already have a bitcoin wallet and don't mind the fees, many platforms allow you to buy coins with your debit or credit card. However, if you want to purchase large amounts of coins with no fees, a wire transfer to your exchange is the best option.
