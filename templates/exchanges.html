Exchanges

Getting into trading and investing in Bitcoin or other cryptocurrencies can be intimidating at first. There are stories constantly about scams and people losing money. While a few scams and thieves still float around the web, it has never been so simple to invest in and safely trade in cryptocurrency as it is today.

Your first concern when you start to trade and invest in a cryptocurrency, is safety and security. The following exchanges will suit any use case. However, if you are planning to store large amounts of crypto or hold your assets long term, you should consider storing your coins in a hardware wallet instead of keeping them in your exchange.
